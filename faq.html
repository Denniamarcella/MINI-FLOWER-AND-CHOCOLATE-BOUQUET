Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • 1. What type of wrapping paper is best for making the bouquet?

    It's best to use sturdy wrapping paper that holds its shape well, such as craft paper, floral wrapping paper, or cellophane.

  • 2. How do I attach the stick securely to the chocolate?

    Use strong adhesive tape, like double-sided tape or floral tape, to ensure the stick is firmly attached to the chocolate.

  • 3. Can I use fresh flowers instead of artificial ones?

    Yes, but fresh flowers will require different care and may need a water source, which can make the bouquet heavier and more complex to assemble.

  • 4. How do I layer the wrapping paper around the chocolate?

    Start by placing one sheet at a slight angle and wrap it around the chocolates, then layer additional sheets slightly offset to create a full, layered look.

  • 5. What does it mean to fold the wrapping paper into an ‘M’ shape?

    Fold the paper so it creates a zigzag pattern resembling the letter 'M'. This helps to add volume and shape to the bouquet.
